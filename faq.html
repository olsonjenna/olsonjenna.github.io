FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW LONG DOES THE PROCESS TAKE?

usually the design process takes about a month from inquiry to order, but we can work as quickly (or slowly) as you need.

HOW LONG DOES IT TAKE TO SHIP?

it takes 7 to 12 business days from order to in-person pickup. it takes 14 to 20 business days from order to deliver via shipment.

HOW CAN I GET IN CONTACT WITH YOU?

visit our CONTACT page or our INQUIRE page.

WHERE DO YOU SHIP?

currently, we only work with clients inside the united states.

WHAT IS THE PRICING? WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE PRICING?

our custom wedding invitations typically range from $500 to $1,500. due to the custom nature of our business, prices vary depending on quantity, paper choice, envelope choice, & specialty items. pricing includes invitations, inserts, envelopes, & photos. other custom event invitations typically range from $400 to $800. pricing includes invitations, inserts, & envelopes.

CAN I PURCHASE DIGITAL INVITATIONS?

no. JO firmly believes that print is not dead.

CAN I ORDER IN A FOREIGN LANGUAGE?

yes. CONTACT us to find out if your language is available.

CAN I RETURN MY ORDER?

due to the nature of our business, custom invitations cannot be returned.

CAN YOU PRINT ADDRESSES ON MY ENVELOPES?

yes, we can print both sender & receiver addresses on your envelopes. it is an additional $75 for return address printing. it is an additional $100 receiver address printing.

WHEN SHOULD I SEND OUT MY INVITATIONS?

six to eight weeks before your event.

HOW MANY DESIGNERS WORK AT JO?

JO is run solely by Jenna Olson. learn more about her story on our ABOUT page.

DO YOU HAVE A RETAIL LOCATION?

JO does not have a public studio, but we hope to open one in the future!

DOES JO PRINT IN-HOUSE?

JO partners with the best local print shops.

DO JO PRODUCTS EVER GO ON SALE?

JO does not hold sales.
