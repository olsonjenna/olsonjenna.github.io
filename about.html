ABOUT

MEET JO

I’m Jenna & I am so glad you are here! Being from the golden coast, I am a lover of all things beautiful & bright. I am a lover of life and believe it deserves to be celebrated. I am crazy about amazing fonts, gorgeous paper & meaningful wording. I cannot wait to work with you & design the invitation of your dreams!

OUR STORY

The idea for JO was born in the summer of 2021 when I decided to marry the love of my life! After scouring the internet, discovering the vast industry of online invitation boutiques, & failing to find the wedding invitation of my dreams, I designed & printed my perfect wedding suite. I started JO because everyone deserves a gorgeous invitation that matches their style & needs. The rest is history!

OUR MISSION

JO’s mission is to elevate life’s most important moments through intentional communication.

OUR VALUES

We value print. (PRINT IS NOT DEAD!) We value intentional design & gracious language. We value the expansion of an individual’s core relationships. We value refinement, class, politeness, love, & kindness. We value fair pricing because we are mindful of the monetary cost of celebration. We value our planet. We keep it happy & healthy by using recyclable paper. We encourage our clients to recycle our products where possible.
